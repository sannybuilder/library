This command sets the zone's peds, gangs, and cops spawn density in car. It allows gang members to spawn as a driver and sometimes also a passenger in cars and is independent from SET_ZONE_PED_INFO, which spawns the gang on foot. By default, GTA III's type `0`, `1`, or `2` zones are in `gta3.zon` and Vice City's type `2` zones are in `info.zon`. The spawned gang cars use hardcoded models by default if models are not set by SET_GANG_CAR_MODEL. The densities of gangs and cops have a range between `0` and `1000` and they must all add up to less than or equal to 1000 for them to spawn properly. `0` means they take up 0% of the random cars in the area and `1000` means they take up 100%.
