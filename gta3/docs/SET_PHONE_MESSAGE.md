This command turns on the payphone and sets it to the message. The phone rings and exaggerates the movement of the payphone object similar to TURN_PHONE_ON. When the player stands in front of the phone, a small cutscene is played &ndash; the player becomes uncontrollable, widescreen is switched on, the player character performs an animation of picking up the phone, and the message is displayed for `3` seconds. The player character then hangs up the phone, widescreen is switched off, the phone turns off, and the player resumes control.
